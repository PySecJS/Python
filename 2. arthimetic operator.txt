>>> # PRIMITIVE OR ARITHIMETIC OPERATOR

>>> # ADDITION OPERATOR
>>> 2 + 3
5

>>> # SUBTRACTION OPERATOR
>>> 3 - 2
1

>>> # MULTIPLICATION OPERATOR
>>> 2 * 3
6

>>> 5 ** 2
25
